This is my final project for STSO 425. I decided to look at the amount and size of wildfires in Canada and compare it to the weather from that same time.

I decided to look at this because wildfires in Canada got bad enough for the smoke to effect New York and it didn't used to do that. So it was a topic that was on my mind.
