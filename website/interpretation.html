Looking at the graphs I have there seems to be a link between the amount of fires and the temperature. Higher average temps lead to more fires. One thing I noticed is that lower avgerage temperature and large amount of damage done by the fires line up suprisingly closely.

At first I thought that it might be because the large fires meant that the staff taking the data would have to leave, causing a void in data during the hotter summer months. However looking at the null data that is not the case.My parents suggested that it might be that the smoke and ash from the fires led to lower tempratures but I just don't have the data to figure out if thats the case.
