This is my final project for STSO 425. I decided to look at the number and size of wildfires in Canada and compare that to the weather from that same time.

I decided to look at this because in 2023 and 2024 wildfires in Canada got bad enough for the smoke to affect New York, and it didn't used to do that. So it was a topic that was on my mind.

How I got and processed my data is on the sources page
